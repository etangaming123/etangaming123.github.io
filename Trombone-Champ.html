Trombone Champ

My custom trombone champ songs! These can be added to Trombone Champ directly if you're on the latest version.

Note: Trombone Champ will have a Steam Workshop feature soon (bluesky post) so these charts will also be uploaded to there once the Steam Workshop update for Trombone Champ releases. (the google drive links will remain, though!)
Update: Trombone Champ released the beta for Steam Workshop uploads, the links to these charts (via steam workshop) will be added here once the Trombone workshop feature has been fully developed (i.e out of beta!)

How to Install

> Download the entire folder (not just the contents, the folder itself)
-> Or just download all the contents and put them in a folder
> Place the folder in your custom songs directory
> Press the reload button on the custom song location within Trombone Champ (or relaunch Trombone Champ)
> All done! Enjoy your new song!!
